Our Story

The idea of creating food truck business came across our minds when we first experienced the happiness brought from the other food truck. It offers a lot of menu options from cupcakes to grilled cheese then to hybrid taco-waffles, and much more foods that are thinkable. As we continue proceeding this business, our food truck now is able to cater most of the customers' tastes or offering gourmet. Our meals gain a good reputation as well.
